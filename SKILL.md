> Built by Khushbu Agrawal | github.com/Khushbu2411/prepmindai

## What is PrepMindAI?
A personal, AI-powered interview prep knowledge base covering DSA, Golang, System Design, and DBMS. Built from real interview experiences across Google, McKinsey, Pocketful, Kyndryl, and more.

## How to use:
- **Quick revision** → go to the pattern/topic you need
- **Before interview** → check the "one-liner" at the bottom of each note
- **Stuck on a problem** → check hints without spoiling the solution
- **Add your own notes** → each section has a "My Notes" area
